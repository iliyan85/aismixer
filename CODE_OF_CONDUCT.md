# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

The aismixer community is committed to a respectful, practical, and
collaborative environment.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- being considerate and constructive;
- discussing technical disagreements with evidence and patience;
- respecting different experience levels and backgrounds;
- accepting feedback and correcting mistakes;
- keeping discussions focused on improving the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks,
threats, deliberate disruption, or publishing another person's private
information without permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews,
discussions, and other project spaces. It also applies when someone is publicly
representing the project.

## Reporting And Enforcement

Report Code of Conduct concerns through a private contact method listed by the
project maintainer. Do not open a public issue when doing so would expose
sensitive or personal information.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate. Reports
will be reviewed as fairly and confidentially as practical.
